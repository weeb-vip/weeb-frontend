Synopsis

The adventure is over, but life goes on for an elf mage just beginning to learn what living is all about. Elf mage Frieren and her courageous fellow adventurers have defeated the Demon King and brought peace to the land. But Frieren will long outlive the rest of her former party, and in time, the great heroes fade into legend.

Frieren wonders if she truly knows what it means to live and to cherish the time she has. Decades after their victory, Frieren is still unchanged even as the world and its people change around her. Reunited briefly with the now-elderly hero Himmel, she realizes she knows little of the humans she has protected.

After Himmel's death, Frieren sets out on a new journey to confront her past and understand her companions better, all while taking on new apprentices -- young and earnest mage Fern and stoic warrior Stark. Through small moments and grand adventures, Frieren traces her memory of a journey already ended and searches for the meaning of fleeting human lives from her near-immortal perspective.
